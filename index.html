OUR WORK

Black-Scholes
A revolutionary equation in the world of quantitative finance, dealing with the option pricing which won its formulators the Nobel Price for Economics

Option Strategies
A tried and tested combination of options designed to improve profits and reduce losses compared to the naked options

Option Greeks
A set of variables derived from Black Scholes affecting the pricing as well as the movement of the option price in accordance with the changes in the underlying

Trading Systems
Algorithms to generate buy and sell signals and automate trading
